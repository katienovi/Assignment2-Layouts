UNCG Grub!

Ever wondered where the best places to get food around campus are? Look no further than UNCG Grub! This blog is dedicated to finding the tastiest and time-tested eateries all within walking distance of UNCG. We look for decently priced and delicious food so that you get the best bang for your buck. We're also open to new recommendations! Let us know if we missed anything.

While none of these restaurants take flex, they are worth your money.

Tate Street Coffee

Tate Street Coffee is a UNCG staple! Whether you're there to study or grab a quick drink, it's usually packed.

Italian Soda

Sparkling water mixed with flavored syrups.

Cheese Danish

A flaky pastry filled with a mix of cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract.

Peppermint Hot Chocolate

A perfectly warm hot chocolate with peppermint flavoring.

Happy Bowl

A Japanese restaurant located near the Kaplan Center, open from 11:00 am - 9:30 pm on Weekdays.

Orange Chicken

Delicious orange chicken with your choice of rice or noodles, with various vegetables.

Cheese Quesadilla

A grilled tortilla with cheese.

Fried Crispy Noodles

Crispy noodles, usually with a cracker like consistency.

Slices Pizza by Tony

Located on 401 Tate Street, Slices by Tony sells a variety of authentic Italian food all within a minute walk of campus.

Baked Ziti Pizza

Tomato sauce, White sauce, Parmigiano cheese, Basil

Cheese Ravioli

Pasta filled with cheese and topped with tomato sauce.

Cannoli

A shell of pastry dough filled with a sweet ricotta cheese filling
